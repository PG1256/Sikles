Sikles

A magnificent Nepali village with captivating scenery

Location and appearance

Sikles can be found in the Kaski district of Nepal. Kaski district is famous for its mountain range it is said Kaski districts mountain range has 11 profuse mountains! Knowing that we could probably already guess how beautiful the views in Sikles must be if it's district has so many abundant mountains.

Sikles has a captivating appearance. You can see many mountains far and near from this village. An example of one is Annapurna.The tenth highest mountain in the world.

Besides that lets look at the interrior of this magnificent village. There are many homes made from different types of materials and quite risky but lovely paths. Personally, I think that Sikles is a great trekking spot because of the scenery and it's simple paths.

Population

Sikles is mainly filled with the Gurung community but there are also other fewer communities living there aswell. It's estimated that there are about 400 households inside Sikles.

Sikles trek

Sikles is again a exquisite trekking spot.The trek starts at the Sikles village and then ends at the kahphuche lake. Though litteraly speaking it ends once you go back to the Sikles village. If you go in an average speed it's estimated to be for about four days.

The next time you go to Nepal make sure to visit this spectacular village!
